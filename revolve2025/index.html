REVOLVE FESTIVAL 2025

Step into the mirage at Revolve Festival

Revolve returns to Coachella with an elevated, exclusive experience that blends fashion, music, and lifestyle like never before. VIPs, influencers, and celebrities come together for a multi-day celebration of culture, creativity, and unforgettable moments.

Are you ready to experience it?

PROJECT OVERVEIW

Revolve returned to us to redefine their festival presence during Coachella weekend.

We went bigger, better, and bolder than ever. This year, we transformed their footprint into a cultural epicenter, blending fashion, music, and lifestyle into a seamless, immersive experience. From headline performances to interactive moments, every element reinforced Revolve’s position at the forefront of festival culture, creating a weekend-long narrative that resonated with talent, influencers, and audiences alike.

DESIGN FRAMEWORK

To bring Revolve 2025 to life, we collaborated with production, management, technical teams, media partners, and sponsors to translate a bold vision into a seamless experience. Every element of the “Desert Mirage” was thoughtfully crafted-from chrome-meets-organic installations and immersive photo moments to interactive brand activations. Spatial storytelling guided the placement of stages, bars, and sponsor touchpoints, ensuring each moment felt playful, intuitive, and highly shareable, while reinforcing Revolve’s signature balance of exclusivity and cultural relevance.



Key Performance

Developed multiple distinctive concepts that redefined the festival experience, aligning with Revolve’s high standards and festival aesthetics

Explored an extensive range of design approaches, from quick sketches and rough 3D models to fully realized, photorealistic renderings

Designed the main entry moment as a monumental landmark and interactive photo destination, capable of hosting hundreds of guests simultaneously and setting the tone for the festival experience

Leveraged the entry moment’s design as a guiding framework to create cohesive sponsor booth designs, ensuring all elements tied seamlessly into the overarching theme
